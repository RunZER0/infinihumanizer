Step 01
Pick your mode
Open the sidebar and choose a writing mode. Recommended works for most academic documents. Formal suits legal or business prose.
Step 02
Paste your text
Paste your AI-generated content into the left panel. The word counter updates live. Stay within your plan's limit shown in the topbar.
Step 03
Click Humanize
Hit the Humanize button. Your text streams into the right panel word by word as it's processed. No refresh needed.

Ethical use
InfiniHumanizer is designed to help writers improve and naturalise their own drafts — not to bypass academic integrity policies. Always review the output yourself and ensure it reflects your genuine ideas before submission. Using this tool to circumvent your institution's rules is your responsibility as the author.
What we don't store
Text pasted into the editor is sent directly to the model for processing and is not logged or stored on our servers. Your documents remain private. Word-balance data is stored only to enforce your plan limit. For details, see our Privacy Policy.
